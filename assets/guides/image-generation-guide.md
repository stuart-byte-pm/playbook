# Playbook Advisory Group - Image Generation Guide

## Photography Style Guidelines

### Overall Aesthetic
- Natural, authentic documentary-style photography
- Warm, natural lighting with soft shadows
- Candid moments that feel real rather than overly staged
- Lifestyle photography approach with professional subjects
- Color palette: warm earth tones, natural blues, soft industrial grays

## Key Visual Characteristics to Maintain

### Do's ✓
- Emphasize natural moments over posed shots
- Include architectural and built environment contexts
- Mix of close-up portraits and wider environmental shots
- Warm, approachable but professional color palette
- Diverse range of professionals in authentic working scenarios
- Balance between indoor (offices, meeting rooms) and outdoor (sites, buildings) settings
- Show sense of scale, quality, and transformation
- Include subtle industrial/architectural details

### Don'ts ✗
- Avoid sterile corporate stock photo aesthetics
- No overly staged or artificial-looking scenes
- Avoid harsh, artificial lighting
- No generic office clichés (handshakes, pointing at screens)
- Avoid cluttered or chaotic backgrounds
- No overly glossy or retouched looks

## Image Categories Needed

### 1. Hero Images (Homepage)
- Wide, cinematic shots of construction/regeneration projects
- Professional consultants in authentic working environments
- Architectural context that suggests scale and quality

### 2. Service Pages
- Close-up of professionals in advisory/review settings
- Documents, plans, or strategic discussions
- Modern office environments with natural light

### 3. Sector Pages
- Specific to each sector (healthcare buildings, university campuses, industrial facilities, residential developments)
- Mix of exterior architecture and interior professional settings

### 4. About/Team (Future)
- Individual professional portraits with personality
- Small team collaboration shots
- Office environment that feels established but not corporate

### 5. Insights/Blog
- Thoughtful individual portraits for author photos
- Abstract architectural details for article headers
- Environmental shots that suggest expertise and context

## Technical Specifications

### Aspect Ratios
- **Hero images:** 16:9 or 21:9 (ultra-wide)
- **Portrait shots:** 3:4 or 2:3
- **Section backgrounds:** 16:9
- **Thumbnails:** 4:5 or 1:1

### Resolution
- Minimum 2400px on longest edge
- Web-optimized versions at 1920px wide

### Color Grading Notes
- Slightly warm overall tone
- Rich but not oversaturated
- Natural skin tones
- Subtle vignetting acceptable
- Maintain detail in shadows and highlights

## Notes for Consistency

- All images should feel like they're from the same "shoot" or visual family
- Maintain consistent color grading across all images
- Balance between people-focused and environment-focused imagery
- Every image should convey professionalism, intelligence, and calm confidence
- Avoid anything that looks "stock photo" - aim for editorial/documentary quality
